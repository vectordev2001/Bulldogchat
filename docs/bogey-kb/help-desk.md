# Bogey Help Desk

## What Bogey can help with

Bogey is Bulldog's AI assistant. In Bulldog Chat, Bogey can:

- Answer questions about how features work (channels, DMs, meetings, calling, SMS)
- Schedule meetings for you with approval
- Find channels, meetings, and people
- Show you recent errors on your account (diagnostics)
- Explain what a specific error message means
- Escalate issues to the Bulldog support team

## Talking to Bogey

- Click the **Bogey** bubble in the bottom-right of the app.
- Or DM Bogey directly — search "Bogey" in the DM picker.
- Ask in plain English. No commands needed.

## Examples

- "How do I invite someone to a private channel?"
- "Schedule a 30-min meeting with the ops channel tomorrow at 10am"
- "I keep getting errors when I upload files, what's happening?"
- "Show me my recent errors"
- "Cancel the meeting I have with Sarah at 3pm"
- "Who owns the Puget Sound Energy channel?"

## Proposals and approvals

When Bogey does something on your behalf (like scheduling a meeting), it creates a **proposal card**. You review the details and click **Approve** to confirm, or **Edit** to change something first. Nothing happens until you approve.

For meetings, once you approve, the meeting is created immediately and invites go out.

## Diagnostics

If you're hitting an error and don't know why:

- Ask Bogey "show me my recent errors" or "why is upload failing?"
- Bogey shows the last few errors from your account with plain-language explanations.
- If it looks like a bug or infrastructure issue, Bogey will offer to open a support ticket.

## Escalation to support

If Bogey can't solve it:

- Ask "open a support ticket" or "I need a human"
- Bogey collects a summary of what you tried, any error IDs, and your workspace info.
- The ticket goes to Bulldog Ops support.
- You'll get a confirmation with a ticket number.

## Privacy

- Bogey only sees your workspace data that you have permission to see.
- Conversation with Bogey is logged for quality but not used for training.
- Ask Bogey "forget this conversation" and it clears the current session context.

## Feedback

- Any Bogey response has 👍 / 👎 buttons.
- Use them liberally — Bogey improves from real feedback.
- For product bugs or feature requests, ask Bogey to file feedback and it will route it to the product team.
