# Channels in Bulldog Chat

## What are channels?

Channels are group conversations organized around a topic, project, or team. Every channel belongs to your company workspace, and messages posted there stay in the channel history.

## Finding a channel

- Open the sidebar on the left. Channels you belong to appear under **Channels**.
- To search all channels in your workspace, click the search bar at the top and type the channel name. Channels you can join appear even if you haven't joined yet.
- Public channels are open to anyone at your company. Private channels only show up if you've been added.

## Joining a channel

1. Search for the channel by name.
2. Click the channel — you'll see a preview with recent messages.
3. Click **Join Channel** at the top.

## Creating a channel

1. Click the **+** button next to "Channels" in the sidebar.
2. Choose **Create channel**.
3. Enter a name, description, and pick **Public** (anyone can join) or **Private** (invite-only).
4. Click **Create**.

## Inviting people

- Open the channel and click the header at the top.
- Choose **Add members**.
- Search for teammates and click **Add**.

## Channel settings

Channel owners and admins can:
- Rename the channel or update the description
- Change public/private
- Archive the channel when it's no longer needed
- Remove members

## Muting and notifications

- Right-click a channel in the sidebar and choose **Mute** to stop notifications.
- Choose **Notification preferences** to only get pinged for @mentions or specific keywords.

## Archiving

Archiving hides a channel from active lists but preserves the message history. Owners can un-archive at any time.
