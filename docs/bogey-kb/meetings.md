# Meetings in Bulldog Chat

## Starting an instant meeting (huddle)

1. Open any channel or DM.
2. Click the **Huddle** or **Start meeting** button at the top of the conversation.
3. Everyone in the conversation can join by clicking the same button.
4. Huddles support voice, video, and screen share.

Huddles are best for quick sync-ups. They end when everyone leaves.

## Scheduling a meeting

1. In a channel or DM, click **+** in the message composer, then choose **Schedule meeting**.
2. Enter title, start time, and end time.
3. Optionally add notes or an agenda.
4. Invite people by picking channel members, or by adding phone numbers/emails for external guests.
5. Click **Schedule**.

A meeting card appears in the conversation with a **Join** button. Everyone invited gets a notification at the start time.

## Joining a scheduled meeting

- Click the **Join** button on the meeting card in the conversation.
- Or click the meeting in your **Upcoming meetings** panel (top of the sidebar).
- Guests can join via SMS or email link — no Bulldog account required.

## In-meeting controls

- Mute/unmute your microphone
- Turn camera on/off
- Share your screen (choose a window, tab, or entire screen)
- Raise hand
- Open chat sidebar
- Record the meeting (owner and admins only)

## Recording and transcripts

- Click **Record** during a meeting. Everyone sees a red recording indicator.
- After the meeting, the recording and AI-generated transcript appear in the original conversation.
- Meeting Clerk automatically summarizes action items and decisions.

## Meeting quality

- Green bars = good connection
- Yellow = degraded — try turning off your camera
- Red = poor — you may drop; switch networks if possible

## SMS/phone dial-in

For external guests without Bulldog:
- Add their phone number when scheduling.
- They'll receive an SMS with a dial-in number and PIN.
- They can also join via a web link.

## Teams and Zoom interop

Bulldog meetings can bridge to Microsoft Teams and Zoom via the Meeting Bridge. When enabled, Teams/Zoom guests appear alongside Bulldog attendees. Ask your admin if the bridge is available for your workspace.
