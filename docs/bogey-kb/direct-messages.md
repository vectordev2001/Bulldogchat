# Direct Messages (DMs) in Bulldog Chat

## What is a DM?

A direct message is a private conversation between you and one or more specific people. DMs don't appear in any channel and aren't visible to anyone outside the conversation.

## Starting a DM

1. Click the **+** button next to "Direct Messages" in the sidebar.
2. Search for the person by name or email.
3. Click their name — the DM opens immediately.
4. Type your message and press Enter.

## Group DMs

To message multiple people at once without creating a channel:

1. Click **+** next to "Direct Messages".
2. Add multiple people (up to 8).
3. Send your first message — the group DM appears in your sidebar with all participants' names.

Group DMs are best for short-term coordination. If the conversation is ongoing or others need to join later, create a private channel instead.

## Files, images, and voice notes

- Drag any file into the DM window to upload it.
- Click the paperclip icon to browse and attach.
- Click the microphone icon to record a voice note.
- Images and videos preview inline.

## Read receipts and typing indicators

- A small avatar under your last message shows the recipient has read it.
- You'll see "typing…" when the other person is composing a reply.

## Muting a DM

Right-click the DM in the sidebar and choose **Mute** to stop notifications. The DM stays in your sidebar but you won't get pinged for new messages.

## Deleting messages

- Hover over a message you sent → click the **⋯** menu → **Delete**.
- Deleted messages are removed for everyone in the DM.
- You can only delete your own messages.
