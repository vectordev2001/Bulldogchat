# Scheduling meetings in Bulldog Chat

## Quick schedule (Bogey)

Ask Bogey to schedule a meeting for you. Just say what you need:

- "Schedule a 30 minute meeting with the ops channel tomorrow at 10am"
- "Set up a call with Sarah and Mike next Friday afternoon about the Puget Sound contract"
- "Book a huddle for the sales team on Monday at 2pm PT for 45 minutes"

Bogey will propose a meeting card. Review the details, then click **Approve** to create it or **Edit** to change anything first.

## What Bogey needs

- **Title** — a short description ("Q3 planning review")
- **When** — start and end time (Bogey will ask if you leave this vague)
- **Who** — a channel, specific people, or a mix. Bogey can also add external guests by phone or email.

If any of these are missing, Bogey will ask. You can also just say "figure out a good time" if the attendees have Bulldog calendars connected.

## Manually scheduling

1. In the channel or DM, click **+** in the composer → **Schedule meeting**.
2. Fill in the details.
3. Click **Schedule**.

## Time zones

- All times in the composer default to your workspace time zone.
- Guests see the meeting time in their own time zone automatically.
- To schedule in a different time zone, type it explicitly: "3pm ET" or "10am PST".

## Recurring meetings

Recurring meetings can be set up in the schedule dialog:
- Daily, weekly (choose days), monthly, or custom cadence
- End date or number of occurrences
- Skip a single occurrence without cancelling the series

## Editing or cancelling

- Click the meeting card in the conversation.
- Choose **Edit** to change the time, title, or attendees.
- Choose **Cancel meeting** to remove it. Everyone invited gets a notification.

## Availability

- Bulldog shows availability from connected Google, Outlook, and Microsoft 365 calendars.
- Ask Bogey "when is Sarah free next week?" and Bogey checks calendars you have permission to see.
- If someone isn't connected, Bogey will say so and suggest asking them directly.

## Bogey approval flow

Every meeting Bogey creates goes through an approval step:

1. You ask Bogey to schedule
2. Bogey shows a card with title, time, attendees, and notes
3. You click **Approve** → meeting is created and invites go out
4. Or **Edit** to tweak, then approve

You can also tell Bogey "just do it" for future meetings and it will remember your preference for that conversation.
